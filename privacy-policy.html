Privacy Policy for PeptidePal

Last Updated: December 16, 2024

Overview

PeptidePal ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you provide it to us directly. The app may collect:

  • Device Identifier: A unique, anonymous device ID to isolate your data on your device
  • Health Data: Weight measurements and progress photos you choose to log (stored locally and in your private Firebase account)
  • Peptide Information: Details about peptides, doses, and schedules you enter

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and support
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data to improve app stability (if enabled)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide core app functionality (tracking peptides, scheduling doses, etc.)
  • Sync your data across your devices using Firebase
  • Send dose reminders and notifications (with your permission)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Process subscription payments through RevenueCat and Apple

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Sync: Data is synced to Firebase using your unique device ID for isolation
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
  • No Account Required: We don't require email or account creation, enhancing privacy

Third-Party Services

PeptidePal uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For data synchronization and storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple In-App Purchase: For payment processing. Apple Privacy Policy

Your Rights and Choices

  • Data Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Data Export: Export your data anytime through the Settings menu
  • Data Deletion: Clear all data through Settings > Clear All Data
  • Notifications: Control notifications through iOS Settings
  • Photo Access: Grant or revoke photo library access anytime

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device and in your Firebase storage indefinitely until you choose to delete it. If you uninstall the app, local data is removed, but Firebase data persists for reinstallation unless you clear it first.

Children's Privacy

PeptidePal is not intended for use by children under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17.

International Users

PeptidePal is designed for use in the United States. If you use the app from other countries, please be aware that data may be transferred to and stored in the United States.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or App Store update notes.

Email: support@peptidepal.app

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA. You may request information about data collection and exercise your rights by contacting us.

Consent
